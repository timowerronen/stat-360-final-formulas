# STAT 360 Final Exam Cheatsheet
A definition &amp; formula sheet for the Washington State University STAT 360 final exam. 
This was made for the section taught in Fall 2025 by Jackie Carlton-Wargo, and may or **may not be applicable to other sections.**

To download this cheatsheet, go to [the PDF](Stats_Final_Formulas.pdf) and click the download button.

I tailored this cheatsheet to myself and my own knowledge of the course material, and uploaded it here in case someone will find it useful. **I do not recommend using it as-is without taking some time to understand it.** I have included the LaTeX file, so you are free to make changes.
